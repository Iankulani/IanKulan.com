Hello, I'm Ian Carter Kulani

A passionate Cybersecurity Specialist & Full Stack Developer with over 10+ years of experience designing secure, high-performance web and mobile applications. I specialize in penetration testing, threat analysis, and cyber drill simulations to strengthen organizational defenses. Leveraging modern JavaScript frameworks, cloud security solutions, and advanced encryption practices, I create systems that are not only functional but also resilient against evolving cyber threats.

My journey began with a Computer Science degree, but my real education came from countless hours of building, breaking, and rebuilding applications to push the limits of what’s possible. I’ve enhanced my expertise with certifications like CompTIA Security+ and specialized training from ITU, equipping me with advanced skills in cybersecurity, penetration testing, and network defense. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science at the University of East London, further deepening my knowledge in cutting-edge technologies. I believe in crafting clean, efficient code that not only works flawlessly but also tells a story of innovation, security, and impact.

When I’m not hardening networks or crafting secure applications, you’ll find me exploring emerging technologies, contributing to open-source security tools, or mentoring aspiring developers. Outside the digital battlefield, I enjoy playing basketball, diving into immersive video games, and tackling new challenges—always ready to collaborate on innovative and impactful projects..
